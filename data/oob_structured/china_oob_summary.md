## Gaps & Uncertainties

### Commander gaps (null or uncertain)
- **Southern TC Commander**: Gen. Wu Yanan reported absent from key events Nov 2025; possible investigation per CNA 2025-11-19. No confirmed replacement named. Marked `[POST-PURGE STATUS UNCERTAIN]`.
- **Western TC Commander**: Gen. Wang Haijiang similarly absent; possible investigation. No confirmed replacement.
- **PLARF Commander**: Gen. Wang Houbin expelled 17 October 2025 for "serious violations of Party discipline" (CMSI Note 17, Oct 2025). Replacement name not confirmed in available sources. All PLARF units carry `[POST-PURGE STATUS UNCERTAIN]` flag.
- **South Sea Fleet**: Two senior STCN commanders (Li Pengcheng, Ju Xinchun) purged 2025; no confirmed replacements. SSF Navy command continuity uncertain.
- **Eastern TC Political Commissar**: Liu Qingsong reportedly under investigation (CNA Nov 2025); Lin Xiangyang (previous TC commander) expelled at 4th Plenum.

### Structural/OOB gaps

#### PLAA Group Armies — brigade numbers not confirmed for all
- **72nd GA** (Huzhou): Wikipedia lists 6 brigade types (1 armored, 2 mech inf, 1 inf, 2 amphibious) but specific numbered designations (e.g., 5th/10th/34th CAB from 2017 reference) not confirmed post-2020 in primary sources. Only typed descriptions available. Third-tier entries omitted for 72nd GA to avoid fabrication.
- **73rd GA** (Xiamen): Amphibious brigade numbers not publicly confirmed (only "2 amphibious brigades" per 2024 Wikipedia). Included as generic amphibious CABs with `_1`/`_2` suffix.
- **79th GA** (Liaoyang): Brigade numbers absent from Wikipedia; only types (4 armored, 1 mech inf, 1 inf) listed. Third-tier entries omitted.
- **75th GA**: Brigade numbers 31st, 32nd, 37th, 42nd, 121st, 122nd, 123rd confirmed by Wikipedia; included.
- **77th GA HQ**: Historically Chongqing (13th GA); US Army SSI 2024 calls HQ "Chongzhou, near Chengdu" — slight inconsistency; Chengdu used in JSON.

#### PLARF Base 63 brigade third-tier gaps
- Only 631st Bde included in third_tier with confirmed data (DF-5B, 6 silos, Jingzhou). Brigades 632nd–636th have confirmed HQ cities and missile systems in the research data but were omitted from the JSON third_tier to keep entries to well-sourced ~50-80 range. All data is in the CNS 2023 report and can be added.

#### PLAAF Brigade gaps
- **WTC PLAAF**: Only 97th (Dazu) and 111th (Korla) J-20 brigades confirmed. Other WTC fighter brigades (J-10, J-11 types) at Urumqi, Lanzhou, Lhasa, Kunming bases are not brigadized-with-numbers in public sources — bases confirmed, brigade numbers not.
- **NTC PLAAF**: Only 1st, 15th, 55th brigades in publicly available brigade-number data. Additional brigades at Jinan, Dalian, Changchun are confirmed by base infrastructure but not individually numbered in open sources.
- **PLAAF Bomber force**: Beyond 8th Bomber Division, PLAAF operates H-6K/H-6N units likely at additional bases (Neixiang, Sanmenxia corridor); specific division/brigade designations for non-8th formations not confirmed in consulted sources.
- **Transport aviation**: PLAAF transport brigades at Xi'an (Y-20 units) and Wuhan not individually numbered in consulted sources; excluded.

#### PLAN Flotilla-level gaps
- Fleet-to-flotilla mapping: PLAN reorganized into Maritime Operations Sub-Centers (MOSCs) under theater commands post-2016 (USNWC CMSI sub report). The old numbered flotillas (1st, 2nd flotilla etc.) no longer appear as clearly labeled in open sources. Flotillas described functionally (submarine, surface) rather than by numbered designation.
- Exact SSN/SSBN counts per fleet are classified; open-source estimates used (12 nuclear total per Wikipedia, ~68 total submarines).

#### PLASSF post-April 2024 structure
- Only three successor organizations confirmed (Aerospace Force, Cyberspace Force, Information Support Force) per Air University CASI Apr 2024. Their exact internal structure (departments, subordinate units) is not yet in open sources. All three entered as single entries; no sub-unit third-tier possible without fabrication.
- Joint Logistics Support Force (JLSF) was pre-existing and not part of PLASSF; not included in this OOB.

#### 2023–2025 Purge impact on readiness
- The PLA experienced its worst corruption purge in decades (2023–2025), affecting:
  - Two PLARF commanders (Li Yuchao 2023, Wang Houbin Oct 2025)
  - ETC Commander Lin Xiangyang (Nov 2025)
  - CMC Vice Chairman He Weidong (Oct 2025)
  - CMC Political Work Dept Director Miao Hua (Oct 2025)
  - Multiple PLAN senior officers
- The operational readiness impact of these purges is unknown but potentially significant. `strength_pct` left at 100 for all PLARF units absent specific open-source attrition data; analyst-grade sources have not published quantified readiness degradation figures.

#### PLAAF Airborne Corps — brigades 130th/131st theater assignments unknown
- 130th and 131st Airborne Brigades confirmed by IISS 2025 but theater command wartime assignments not specified in public sources; excluded from third_tier pending further sourcing.
